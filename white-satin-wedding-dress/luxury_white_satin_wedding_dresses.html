Additional Costs to Consider

When budgeting for a luxury wedding dress, remember to account for these additional expenses:

  • Alterations: $500-$1,500 depending on complexity
  • Accessories: Veil ($300-$1,200), headpiece ($200-$800), shoes ($200-$1,000)
  • Preservation: Professional cleaning and preservation ($550-$1,200)

Shopping Recommendations

  • Schedule appointments at luxury bridal salons that carry these designers
  • Consider trunk shows which often feature special pricing and the opportunity to meet designers
  • Allow 6-9 months for ordering as most luxury gowns are made to order
  • Budget for alterations which can add $500-$1,500 to the final cost
  • Explore sample sales from luxury designers, which can offer significant discounts
