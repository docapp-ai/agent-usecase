Flight Booking Recommendations

  • Book 3-4 months in advance for best prices in August (high season)
  • With young children, direct flights are strongly recommended despite higher cost
  • Request seats together during booking (some airlines charge extra for seat selection)
  • Check baggage allowances before booking (typically 2 checked bags per person on international flights)
  • Consider arrival time in Seattle to minimize jet lag impact on children

Top Attractions for Kids (6-9 years)

Must-Visit Attractions
  • Pacific Science Center - Interactive science exhibits
  • Woodland Park Zoo - Award-winning zoo with 300+ species
  • Museum of Flight - World's largest air and space museum
  • Pike Place Market - Historic market with flying fish
  • Space Needle - Iconic tower with glass floor
Great for Nice Weather
  • Alki Beach - Beach with views of downtown
  • Washington State Ferries - Scenic boat rides
  • Discovery Park - Largest city park with trails
  • Seattle Great Wheel - Waterfront Ferris wheel
  • Fremont Troll - Quirky sculpture kids can climb
Rainy Day Options
  • Seattle Aquarium - Marine life with touch pools
  • Museum of Pop Culture - Interactive music exhibits
  • Museum of Illusions - Mind-bending exhibits
  • Seattle Children's Museum - Play-based activities
  • Kids Discovery Museum - On Bainbridge Island

Money-Saving Tip

Consider the Seattle CityPASS if you plan to visit multiple attractions. It includes:

  • Space Needle (2 visits in 24 hours)
  • Seattle Aquarium
  • Choice of: Museum of Pop Culture OR Woodland Park Zoo
  • Choice of: Pacific Science Center OR Chihuly Garden and Glass
  • Choice of: Harbor Cruise OR Museum of Flight

Price: Adults: $115 | Children (5-12): $89 - Saves up to 49% on attraction tickets

Getting Around Seattle

Public Transportation
  • Link Light Rail: Connects airport to downtown ($3-3.25)
  • King County Metro Buses: Extensive network ($2.75)
  • Monorail: Seattle Center to downtown ($3.00)
  • Streetcars: Limited routes in certain neighborhoods
  • ORCA Card: Reloadable card for all transit systems
Other Options
  • Rideshare: Uber/Lyft widely available
  • Taxis: Available at airport and major hotels
  • Water Taxi: Downtown to West Seattle
  • Ferries: To islands in Puget Sound
  • Rental Car: Not recommended for city stay

Transportation Tips for Families

  • Airport to Hotel: After a long flight with children, consider taking a taxi/rideshare from the airport despite higher cost
  • Choose Central Accommodation: Staying near Seattle Center or downtown minimizes transit needs
  • ORCA Cards: Children under 6 ride free; reduced fares for ages 6-18
  • Plan Buffer Time: Allow extra time when using public transportation with children
  • Rideshare Apps: Often the most convenient option for families with young children

Money

Communication
